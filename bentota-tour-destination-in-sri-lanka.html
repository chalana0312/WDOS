Bentota - Sri Lanka

Bentota is a tourist attraction, with a handful of world-class hotels. It is a destination for water sports. Bentota also delivers an ancient art of healing called Ayurveda. Bentota is famous for its toddy production, an alcoholic beverage made out of coconut nectar.

Major Attractions

  • Water sports on the Bentota river.
  • Ayurvedic treatments.
  • Apart from swimming, body-surfing or diving in the sea, you can jet-ski, windsurf, parasail or enjoy a romantic river safari on the river and lagoon.

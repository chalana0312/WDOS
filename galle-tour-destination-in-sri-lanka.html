Galle - Sri Lanka

Galle is a major city in Sri Lanka, situated on the southwestern tip of Sri Lanka, 119 km from Colombo. This city has much to offer you. Among major attractions are Mirissa and Unawatuna swimming beach with bars, restaurants . Mirissa whale watching.

Major Attractions

  • Ayurvedic Treatments Centre – Weligama
  • Kosgoda Turtle hatchery.
  • Sinharaja Forest Reserve- Kottawa – hiking.
  • Fishing
  • Underwater diving , snorkelling at Hikkaduwa.
  • Visiting Dutch Fortress and discovering the Fort.
  • Ahangama surf beach ideal for intermediate surfers.
  • Spectacular Koggala lake boat trip to Buddhist monasteries.

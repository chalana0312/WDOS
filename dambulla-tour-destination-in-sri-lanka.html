Dabulla/Sigiriya - Sri Lanka

Major Attractions

  • Rock cave temple where 40 Buddhist statues are enshrined.(Unesco –World Heritage Site).
  • En-route Dambulla , spice gardens and their products.
  • Pinnawela Elephant Orphanage.
  • Sigiriya royal palace of an ancient Sinhalese king at an elevation of about 200 metres (Unesco –World Heritage Site)

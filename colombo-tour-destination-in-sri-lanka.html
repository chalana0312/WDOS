Colombo - Sri Lanka

Commercial capital of Sri Lanka, Colombo has a wide range of attractions waiting for you. All the most important government institutions and commercial buildings including class hotels are concentrated here.

Major Attractions

Busiest Fort bazaar and old English buildings , Fort Railway Station, Dutch museum , Colombo Harbour, hotels area, national museum, Viharamahadevi Park, (old Victoria Park) residential area at Cinnamon Gardens, Kocchikade Kovil world trade centre, shopping complexes , Gangarama temple, various kinds of restaurants offering international cuisine, night life, discos, casino clubs, island in the Beira Lake, new parliament building surrounded by a river, Galle face Terrace, Dehiwala zoo, Nelum Pokuna national performance art theatre.

Shopping Complexes

Majestic city- Odel- Liberty Plaza- Fashion House, Food city Laksala to name a few.

Beaches

The nearest swimming beach is Mount Lavinia Beach a few kilometres off. If you like a rich lunch buffet, please visit International buffet at Hotel Cinnamon Grand Hotel.

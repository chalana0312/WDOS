Nuwara-Eliya - Sri Lanka

The highest city of the country at an elevation of about 2400 metres. The way to this particular city is through lush vegetations, spectacular tea plantations, waterfalls , tea factories, valleys etc. Some tourists like to take the hill country railway line from Nanu Oya to Ella in order to observe the beauty of this valleys and highlands as the train passes through them in close proximity.

Major Attractions

  • Victoria park, Hakgala Flower Garden, Ramboda Falls, Ambewela Farm Horton’s Place (world’s end), Houses, hotels and government,Institutions in British architecture.
  • Viewing the Ella gap.

Kataragama - Sri Lanka

Kataragama a multi-religious sacred city is a small town with clean, tree lined roads with rows of stalls selling garlands & platters of fruit-coconut, mango , maize, vegetables and watermelon.

Major Attractions

  • Visiting the holy premises to observe religious ceremonies .
  • Yala Jeep Safari Tour
